About

This fan-made website is based on the popular pirate game "Sea Of Thieves," developed by Rare and published by Xbox Game Studios. The goal of this website was to make a community run "hub" in which players could learn more about the game, share their experiences, and form parties. This website does not make any profit (as that would be illegal), nor do we claim any of the rights to the game. This was simply made out of passion.
